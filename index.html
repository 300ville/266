Inventory and Fixed Asset Interest Allocation Estimator

Background

The Section 266 Interest Recovery Calculator estimates interest potentially allocable to inventory and fixed assets using average annual debt, interest expense, and expenditure data.

The tool calculates the weighted average interest rate, estimated interest allocable to inventory and fixed assets, and the portion of inventory interest allocated to ending inventory and cost of goods sold.

This is a preliminary estimator. Actual results depend on the taxpayer’s facts, documentation, capitalization methodology, and applicable limitations, including Section 163(j).

Developed by Jeffrey Kelson, Stanley Barsky, Ben White, and Jaime Garcia de Paredes.

How to use

  1. Enter the average annual debt balance and total annual interest expense.
  2. Enter the average annual direct and indirect inventory expenditures and fixed asset expenditures.
  3. Enter the portion of inventory remaining at year end as a decimal, such as 0.25 for 25 percent.
  4. Select Calculate, review the results, and use Copy Results if needed.
